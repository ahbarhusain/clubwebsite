About Us

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Society of NSUT East Campus

The Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Society of NSUT East Campus provides an outstanding platform for students interested in delving into the exciting fields of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. Our interdisciplinary society welcomes students with a passion for mechanical engineering, electronics, automation, control systems, and software development. We offer a multitude of opportunities to explore the design, construction, programming, and characterization of intelligent systems and machine learning algorithms throughout the year. We firmly believe in collaborative teamwork.

Our common goal is to foster technical awareness and practical skills in the fields of automation and machine learning. In addition to conducting research projects and AI-related competitions, we organize workshops and events to ignite the enthusiasm of our fellow students. At the Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Society, we create a vibrant learning environment with a focus on holistic skill development. We are a closely-knit community dedicated to pushing the boundaries of technology!
